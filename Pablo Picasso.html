A Short Biography of Pablo Picasso:


  1. Picasso was born on October 25, 1881 in the city of Malaga. Spain
  2. His real name is Pablo Diego Jose Francisco de Paula Juan Nepomuceno Maria de los Remedios Cipriano de la Santisima Trinidad Martyr Patricia Clito Ruiz y Picasso
  3. He received this name when he was baptised
  4. His long name comes from a list of saint and relatives
  5. His first word was "lapiz", the spanish word for pencil
  6. His father was an artist, art professor amd museum curator
  7. Pablo completed his first painting when he was nine years old. The title of the painting was Le Picador. The image is of a man riding a horse in a bullfight
  8. His father decided that he would give up painting when Picassowas 13 years old because he felt his son was already a better painter than him
  9. He studied at the royal academy of San Fernando
  10. Picasso was extremely fond of the works of El Greco
  11. In between his Blue and Rose periods, Pablo Picasso moved to France
  12. Picasso's first job was with an art dealer named Pere Menach
  13. Picasso was a co-founder in the movement known as Cubism
  14. The Nazis would not let Picassodisplay his paintings in Germany
  15. Picasso's art work can be found in art museums throughtout the world
  16. Picasso had many lovers throughout his life. He often painted pictures pf pne of his many lovers, named Dora Maar
  17. Picasso had four different children by three different women
  18. He was married twice
  19. Picasso passed away on April 8, 1973 in Mougins, France
